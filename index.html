Ideas for reducing waste at Home

  • Bring reusable bags and containers with you when you shop, travel, or pack foods.
  • Use water bottles,coffee mugs,plates,etc.made of reusable material.
  • Start composting organic kitchen waste into fertilizers.
  • Pay your bills online. Going paperless will save trees.
  • Go paperless in the kitchen too. Use reuasable clothes instead of paper towels.
  • Use products made of bamboo.
  • Donate your old goods like clothing, furniture, books, and many more.
  • Say no to plastic straws.
  • Delete spam e-mails time to time as they pollute the environment.
  • Use biodegradable items.
  • Focus on segregating and disposing off waste properly and sell the saleable scrap to local kabaadiwalas.
